Compared to previous cycles dominated by awe at raw model capabilities, the current focus is distinctly pragmatic and critical. Developers are demanding better transparency, pricing consistency, and safety sandboxes, showing a maturing user base that is moving past the "magic" phase of LLMs into the hard realities of integration, cost management, and trust verification.
