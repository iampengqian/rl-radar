## 3. Ecosystem Signal
The current ecosystem is defined by the rapid convergence of massive scale and ruthless efficiency. Mixture-of-Experts (MoE) architectures, like Qwen's 35B-A3B and NVIDIA's 30B-A3B, have become standard, offering 30B+ level performance while keeping active parameters and compute costs low. The open-source vs. proprietary battle continues to skew heavily toward open-weights, but with a new twist: the community is aggressively "abliterating" (uncensoring) top-tier proprietary releases like Gemma-4. This has led to an explosion of uncensored fine-tunes, indicating strong friction between corporate safety guardrails and end-user preferences. Unsurprisingly, quantization engines like Unsloth and MLX are riding this wave, driving millions of downloads by making these monstrous 30B-1T parameter models accessible on consumer hardware.
