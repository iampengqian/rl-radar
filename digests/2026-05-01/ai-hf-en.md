### 3. Ecosystem Signal
The current ecosystem signals a definitive transition from standard dense LLMs to highly efficient Mixture-of-Experts (MoE) and multimodal networks. The Qwen3.6 family is dominating download charts, largely because its MoE architecture (like the 35B-A3B) allows heavyweight performance at highly accessible computational costs. Open-weight models are aggressively outpacing proprietary ones in community engagement, driven by rapid grassroots ecosystem support. Unsloth's GGUF quantizations and HauhauCS's "Uncensored" fine-tunes are racking up millions of downloads, proving that local deployment and alignment removal remain primary use cases for open-source developers. Furthermore, the definition of a standard "language model" is evolving into an omni-modal interface; unified any-to-any models like Nvidia's Nemotron-3 and LLaDA2.0 indicate that future open-weights will natively handle vision, audio, and text simultaneously out of the box. Base model releases (like DeepSeek-V4-Pro-Base) being made available in FP8 format also highlights a maturing focus on precision-optimized training foundations.
